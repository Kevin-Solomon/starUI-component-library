Button

Buttons refer to styled HTML buttons that developers customize to match their website designs. You can manipulate the colors, text sizes, padding, and even change styling properties when buttons enter different states.

Contained Button

Contained buttons can also be considered as the primary button of your website.

Outline Button

Outline buttons can also be considered as the secondary button of your website.
